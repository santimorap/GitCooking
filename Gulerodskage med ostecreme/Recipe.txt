Gulerodskage med ostecreme

	
Recipe:
Stir the flour and oil together, beat in the eggs one at a time, then mix the dry ingredients and pour / stir in the grated carrot.

Pour into a baking pan 23x33 cm. and baked at 185 degrees C. alm. oven for 45 minutes.

Cream cheese : Mix
cream cheese, butter, icing sugar and vanilla sugar together.

Spread the cream cheese on top of the cake when it has cooled.

Tips:
Cream cheese is not suitable for freezing.

...Enjoy! :)
